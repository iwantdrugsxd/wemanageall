# Feature Ideas for Personal Life OS

## üéØ Core Philosophy
Your app is a "Personal Life OS" - a calm, non-intimidating space for managing life. All features should follow:
- **Low friction** - Easy to use
- **Contextual** - Tied to your life
- **Non-overwhelming** - No clutter
- **Stateful** - Remembers everything

---

## üìä Analytics & Insights (High Value)

### 1. **Personal Dashboard Analytics**
- **Weekly/Monthly Review**: Auto-generated summary of your week/month
- **Habit Streaks**: Track consecutive days for habits
- **Mood Trends**: Visualize mood patterns over time
- **Productivity Insights**: "You're most productive on Tuesdays"
- **Goal Progress**: Visual progress bars for long-term goals
- **Time Allocation**: See where your time actually goes

### 2. **Money Insights**
- **Spending Trends**: "You spent 20% more on dining this month"
- **Budget Alerts**: Gentle reminders when approaching limits
- **Savings Goals**: Track progress toward savings targets
- **Category Breakdown**: Pie charts of spending categories
- **Income vs Expenses**: Monthly comparison view

### 3. **Project Analytics**
- **Velocity Tracking**: Tasks completed per week
- **Time Estimates vs Actual**: Learn your time estimation accuracy
- **Most Productive Times**: When do you complete most tasks?
- **Project Health Trends**: Track project health over time
