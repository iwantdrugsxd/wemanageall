## ‚ùì FAQ Section

### Section Title
**Questions? We've Got Answers.**

### Questions

#### Q: Is OFA free?
**A:** Yes! OFA is free to start. Create an account and begin organizing your life at no cost. Premium features may be added in the future, but core functionality will always be available.

#### Q: How is this different from other productivity apps?
**A:** Most apps help you do more. OFA helps you do what matters. We connect your identity (values, roles) to your actions (projects, tasks) so you can live intentionally, not just productively.

#### Q: Is my data private?
**A:** Absolutely. Your data is yours. We use encryption, secure storage, and never sell your information. Your thoughts, projects, and personal information stay private.

#### Q: Can I use OFA on mobile?
**A:** Yes! OFA works on all devices. Access your life OS from your phone, tablet, or computer. Your data syncs automatically.

#### Q: How does voice transcription work?
**A:** We use free browser-based speech recognition for real-time transcription. Your voice is processed locally when possible, and we use free APIs for grammar improvements. No expensive subscriptions required.

#### Q: Can I export my data?
**A:** Yes. You own your data and can export it anytime. We believe in data portability and user control.

#### Q: Do you offer support?
**A:** Yes! We're here to help. Contact us through the app or email support@ofa.life. We typically respond within 24 hours.

#### Q: Can I collaborate with others?
**A:** Yes! Share projects with family, friends, or team members. Collaborate on tasks, share lists, and work together toward common goals.

## üé® Content Tone & Voice

### Voice Characteristics
- **Professional but approachable** - Not corporate jargon, but not too casual
- **Confident but humble** - We believe in our solution, but we're not arrogant
- **Clear and direct** - No fluff, just value
- **Empowering** - Focus on what users can achieve, not what they're missing

### Writing Principles
1. **Lead with value** - What's in it for them?
2. **Show, don't just tell** - Use examples and specifics
3. **Address objections** - Anticipate concerns
4. **Create urgency** - But don't be pushy
5. **Build trust** - Be transparent and honest

### Key Messages
- **Intentional over productive** - It's not about doing more
- **Connection over collection** - Everything should link together
- **You over template** - Personalization is key
- **Privacy over profit** - Your data is yours
- **Free over freemium** - Core features are free
